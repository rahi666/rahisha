LIFE & CAREER

Early Life & Pre-Debut (1995–2015): Born in Gunpo, South Korea, Jisoo joined YG Entertainment as a trainee in 2011. Before her official debut, she built experience by starring in music videos for Epik High and HI Suhyun, and making a cameo in the drama The Producers.

Blackpink & Early Acting (2016–2022): She debuted with Blackpink in August 2016. Alongside group success, she co-hosted Inkigayo, contributed songwriting credits to "Lovesick Girls" and "Yeah Yeah Yeah," and set major viral beauty trends. She made her lead acting debut in the drama Snowdrop (2021), winning Outstanding Korean Actress at the Seoul International Drama Awards.

Solo Debut, Independence, and Global Honors (2023): Jisoo released her debut single album ME in March 2023. The lead track "Flower" became a massive global hit, and the album made her the first K-pop female soloist to sell a million copies in a single week. Later that year, she was invested as an Honorary Member of the Order of the British Empire (MBE) by King Charles III and subsequently founded her independent solo label, Blissoo.

Record-Breaking Music & Film Career (2024–2026): Partnering with Warner Records, she dropped the EP Amortage (2025), pushing her cumulative solo sales past 2 million copies—a second for any K-pop female soloist. Her duet with Zayn, "Eyes Closed," gave her her first solo entry on the Billboard Hot 100. Simultaneously, she expanded her acting profile by starring in the zombie comedy series Newtopia and making her big-screen lead debut in the blockbuster film Omniscient Reader: The Prophecy.

IMPACT

Cultural & Social Dominance: Consistently ranked among South Korea's most popular idols, Jisoo became the most-followed Korean actress on Instagram (surpassing 79.5 million followers). Industry experts have cited her alongside BTS's Jungkook as the modern, global standard of world beauty.

The "Jisoo Effect" in Fashion: As a premier figure shaping the fashion industry (voted in the prestigious BoF 500 list), she was named the top fashion influencer in luxury by Lefty in 2024, generating a staggering €127 million in Earned Media Value (EMV).

Brand Powerhouse:

  • Dior: Named a global ambassador, she directly boosted pandemic-era sales, served as the primary muse for their Autumn/Winter 2021 collection, and even inspired a custom lip balm shade ("Seoul Scarlet"). Her individual Paris Fashion Week posts routinely generate millions in Media Impact Value (MIV), driving up to 45% of the brand's total media traffic.
  • Cartier & Tommy Hilfiger: Drove 45% of Cartier's total EMV in 2022, and single-handedly generated $8 million in EMV for Tommy Hilfiger during the Spring/Summer 2025 New York Fashion Week.

Tech & Gaming: Her creative design collaboration with the mobile game KartRider Rush+ caused the app's player ranking and market popularity to instantly surge upon announcement.
